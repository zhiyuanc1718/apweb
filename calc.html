What does a Limit mean for a function?

The y value that a function approaches when it reaches a certain x value. This is the expected y value for a function, the value does not have to be defined.

Derivatives

Image

What are Derivatives?

The slope of the Tangent line at a specific point is called the derivative. In other words, it is the rate of change at an instantaneous moment. Unlike the average rate of change, finding the slope at one point requires more work, and this is why we need to learn some calculus to find the value.

Integrals

Image

How are Integrals different? Are they something new again?

Integrals help us determine the area under a curve. While derivatives find the change, integrals are antiderivatives: they find what was being differentiated. These terms might sound fancy or confusing but Calculus is here to help you understand.
