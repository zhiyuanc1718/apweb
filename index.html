What Is AP ?

Advanced Placement

AP Courses are tougher than regular courses.

Before consider taking an AP course, remind yourself the effort you will need to show towards the class.

AP is taught at College Level (introductory) and is recommended that you meet ALL of the prerequisites

College Credit

Since AP is college level, many colleges offer credits for these courses.

In order to earn these credits, taking the course is important, but passing the exam itself is the only way to earn college credit.

For any AP exam, a passing score is 3, but we recommend to aim for scores of 4 or 5.
